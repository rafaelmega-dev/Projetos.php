Our Sweet Story
Our Story

Born from three great loves

Tres Amores Ateliê was born from a passion for fine ingredients, meticulous craft, and the joy of sharing sweetness with those we love.

Every cake, truffle, and petit four that leaves our atelier carries the warmth of hands that care. We believe confectionery is an art form — one that deserves patience, the finest chocolate, seasonal fruits, and a generous heart.

From intimate birthday celebrations to grand wedding tables, we create bespoke sweets that become part of your most treasured memories.
